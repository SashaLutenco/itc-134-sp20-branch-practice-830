In this story, a heroic mobster falls passionately in love with an amiable wanderer - all thanks to a flashback.

This story starts in a grocery store in a manufacturing city.

His flashback happend after he got into the hospital. He was hurt while hunting the most wanted person in manufacturing city.

The city is a rough place, even for mobsters. The streets are filled with giant rats and smoked with fumes frm the manufacturing company.

Campers everywhere; nowhere to run. The streets of Tacoma will never be the same.
